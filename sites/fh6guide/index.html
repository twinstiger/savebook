Welcome to Forza Horizon 6 Japan

Forza Horizon 6 drops you into the driver's seat across Japan's breathtaking landscapes — from neon-lit Tokyo city streets to treacherous touge passes carved into mountain forests. This isn't just another open-world racing game; it's a love letter to Japanese car culture, where drift legends are born and touge warriors prove their worth.

Whether you're sliding a Nissan Silvia through a hairpin on Mount Akagi, hunting down a forgotten Toyota 2000GT in a forgotten barn, or competing in the Summer Thunder championship series, FH6Guide is your go-to resource for every aspect of the game.

Pro Tip

Start with our Beginner Guide before jumping into competitive races. Understanding the economy system early will save you hours of grinding later.

What's New in FH6 Japan

The Japan map brings three distinct racing environments: sprawling urban circuits around Shibuya and Yokohama, technical mountain passes inspired by real touge routes like Mount Haruna and Akagi, and high-speed coastal highways. The new drift physics engine delivers unprecedented control, while the updated Photo Mode lets you capture your best moments in stunning detail.

Seasonal Events

Every week brings new challenges across four seasonal categories. Complete championships, hit PR stunt targets, and earn exclusive reward cars that rotate in the Forzathon Shop.

Summer Thunder

Season 1 features aggressive drift events and touge sprints. Low grip, high reward.

Autumn Rally

Technical rally stages through mountain forests. AWD recommended for podium finishes.

Winter Drift

Ice and snow bring new challenges. Soft compound tires and precise throttle control are essential.
