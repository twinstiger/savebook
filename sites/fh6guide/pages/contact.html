Frequently Asked Questions

Can I contribute a guide to FH6Guide?
Yes. We accept guest contributions from players with verified FH6 Japan experience. Submit a rough draft via the contact form and our editorial team will review it. We credit all contributors by name. Guides must meet our editorial standards and be original content.
How often is the site updated?
Pages are updated within 48 hours of major game patches that affect gameplay mechanics, car stats, or track layouts. Seasonal pages are updated each Thursday before the new season begins. Car lists and barn find guides are cross-referenced against game data weekly.
Why doesn't my car's stats match what you listed?
Car stats in FH6 change with game updates, and individual cars may vary based on your specific upgrade configuration. Our car list reflects base showroom stats unless noted. If you believe a stat is incorrect, send us the car name, year, and specific stat, and we'll verify against the current game version.
I found a broken link. What do I do?
Use the contact form and select "Broken Link Report" as the subject. Include the page URL and the specific link text that's broken. We fix broken links within 24 hours of receiving the report.
Do you accept advertising inquiries?
We use Google AdSense for all advertising. If you're a potential AdSense partner with a relevant product or service, you can reach us via the contact form. We maintain strict editorial independence from all advertising relationships.
