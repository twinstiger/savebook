Story Walkthrough

Chapter 1-3 complete walkthrough. Full game = 10 chapters (~10 hours each). No major spoilers beyond each chapter.

⚠️ This walkthrough covers Chapters 1-3. Later chapter details are intentionally vague.
Chapter 1 — Available Now

Arrival on Proteus

~10 hours
Main Objectives
  • Collect basic resources (Titanium, Water Slugs, Copper)
  • Build Scanner → unlock blueprints
  • Unlock Digest ability at Angel Comb
  • Find Welcome Center → power it up
  • Scan Black Box → unlock Biomods
  • Reach Angel Comb → start first base
  • Power up Welcome Center → unlock Dash Biomod
  • Activate first Black Box → progress to Chapter 2
🔑 Scanner Blueprint ⚡ Dash Biomod 🫁 Oxygen Control 🚤 Tadpole Access
Chapter 2 — Available Now

The Ancient Ruins

~10 hours
Main Objectives
  • Build and launch Tadpole from core drone bay
  • Navigate to Bunker signal point (~250m SE)
  • Find and scan second Black Box
  • Explore ancient ruins → unlock Thermal Vision Biomod
  • Purify Bloom Cankers at Wander Signal → unlock Heat Resistance
  • Reach Tadpole Pens → complete Chapter 2
🚤 Tadpole Vehicle 👁️ Thermal Vision 🔥 Heat Resistance 🔮 Ancient Blueprints
Chapter 3 — Available Now

The Deep Claim

~10 hours
Main Objectives
  • Deploy Cyclops from forward base (story trigger)
  • Navigate to deep zones — requires Pressure Adaptation
  • Discover Collector Leviathan territory
  • Final objective at deep ruins → chapter ending
🌊 Pressure Adaptation 🔋 Cyclops Blueprint 📍 Deep Zone Access
