📍 Optimal Base Locations
🌸 Angel Comb (Recommended)

Central location, resource-rich, safe zone. Great first base site. Close to key story objectives.

🏚️ Welcome Center Area

Power infrastructure nearby. Good for mid-game expansion. Moderate threat level.

🕳️ Old Habitat (NW, 420m)

Historical site with rare resources. Higher threat. Best as a forward outpost, not main base.

🐙 Deep Zone (Endgame)

Requires Pressure Adaptation. Only for late-game builds. High risk, huge rewards.
