Before You Write

This wiki covers Subnautica 2 Early Access v1.0. As the game is in active development, some information may change between patches. If you spot outdated content, please let us know — your report helps keep the guide accurate.

For game bugs or glitches, please report directly to Unknown Worlds Entertainment via the official channels rather than here.
