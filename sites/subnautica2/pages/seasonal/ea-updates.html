Track every version of Subnautica 2 Early Access. EA expected to last 2-3 years.

v1.0.0 — May 14, 2026 Launch Day
🎮 Subnautica 2 — Early Access Launch
What's in the Launch Version
  • 3 Chapters available — ~30 hours of main story content
  • 51+ creatures with dynamic AI behavior (UE5 behavior tree)
  • 7 Points of Interest across 2 primary biomes
  • 12 Biomods — abilities to unlock through story progression
  • 4-player Co-op — PC + Xbox cross-platform multiplayer
  • Tadpole vehicle — scout submarine for tight cave navigation
  • Cyclops blueprint — unlocks in Chapter 3
  • Full base building — modules, power systems, decoration
  • Proteus planet — new setting, no connection to original Subnautica
  • Biomod system — replaces original Subnautica's upgrade modules
