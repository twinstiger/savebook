New Player Getting Started Guide

Last Updated: May 2026

Choose Your Platform

2026's gaming platform choices are richer than ever. PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X, Nintendo Switch 2, and high-performance PCs each have their own strengths.

PlayStation 5: Rich exclusive titles, unique DualSense controller experience.

Xbox Series X: Game Pass subscription offers great value, more cross-platform games.

Nintendo Switch 2: Portability and exclusive titles, suitable for casual players.

PC: Maximum flexibility, MOD support, unlimited graphics potential.

Beginner Game Recommendations

Open World Exploration

Forza Horizon 6 Japan and Subnautica 2 are perfect for players who enjoy free exploration.

Rhythm Action

Dead As Disco combines RPG and rhythm gaming, simple to start and fun.

Multiplayer Co-op

It Takes Two and Overcooked are perfect for playing with friends.

Basic Gaming Knowledge

  • Understand the game's save mechanism
  • Familiarize yourself with controller/keyboard operations
  • Learn to check map and quest log
  • Understand basic etiquette for multiplayer gaming

Gaming Budget Advice

It's recommended to divide your gaming budget into three parts: 60% on high-quality AAA titles, 30% to try indie games, 10% reserved for sales. Older games typically have significant discounts 6-12 months after release.
