Game Cheats vs Official Tips: Which is Better?

Last Updated: May 2026

The Golden Age of Game Cheats

During the early days of gaming, cheat codes were essential skills for every player. From Doom's IDDQD to GTA's cheats, these codes let players experience games in unprecedented ways.

Cheat Codes in Modern Games

Today, most AAA titles no longer include built-in cheat codes. However, PC platforms still preserve the tradition of enabling cheat functions through console commands or mods.

Pros and Cons of Using Cheat Codes

Advantages:

  • Unlock game content previously inaccessible
  • Easily overcome difficult sections
  • Freely explore the game's limits
  • Create unique entertainment experiences

Disadvantages:

  • May break game balance
  • Cannot unlock achievements/trophies
  • May corrupt save files
  • Affects game experience completeness

The Value of Official Tips

Our guides provide official tips that don't break game balance. These tips help players overcome difficulties without ruining the gaming experience.

How to Choose

If you just want to complete the game, choose official guides and tips. If you want a completely different experience, or purely casual entertainment, cheat codes are the better choice.
