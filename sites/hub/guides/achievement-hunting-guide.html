Achievement & Trophy Hunting Complete Guide

Last Updated: May 2026

The Charm of Achievement Hunting

Achievements (Steam/PC), Trophies (PlayStation), or Gamerscore (Xbox) are not just virtual badges but proof of your investment of time. Hunting achievements lets you experience games in unprecedented ways.

Achievement Type Analysis

Story Achievements

Achievements naturally unlocked by completing the main story, usually the easiest to obtain. Account for 30-40% of total achievements.

Collection Achievements

Achievements requiring collecting specific items, characters, or resources. Progress tracking is important; multiple playthroughs may be needed.

Skill Achievements

Achievements testing player operation skills, such as no-damage clears or specific combos. Requires extensive practice.

Hidden Achievements

Achievements without displayed conditions, requiring players to discover them independently.

Efficient Collection Strategies

1. Plan Multiple Playthroughs: For multi-playthrough games, plan collection order in advance to reduce repeated playtime.

2. Use Guides: Use achievement guides to understand difficult points and missed items.

3. Create a Checklist: Record obtained and pending achievements, track progress.

4. Note Time Points: Some achievements relate to in-game dates or special events.
