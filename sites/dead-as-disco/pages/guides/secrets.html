Hidden Secrets

Discover every ending, easter egg, and secret room Dead As Disco has to offer.

Secrets & Easter Eggs

Hidden endings, secret songs via My Music, unlock conditions, memorabilia locations, and the mystery behind The Encore restoration.

Hidden Endings

The True Ending requires all collectibles and correct choices

Dead As Disco has three endings. Only the True Ending requires all collectibles and correct story choices:

  • Collect all 50+ memorabilia items across the city
  • Restore The Encore completely (all upgrades)
  • Make the right story choices in each Idol encounter
  • Find the secret track hidden in the Harmony Corp building

My Music — Secret Songs

Import your own tracks to unlock secrets

The My Music feature lets you import your own tracks. Some hidden songs are unlocked by importing specific music:

  • Any track named "Encore" unlocks the secret bar upgrade
  • Tracks over 6 minutes unlock Extended Mix mode in Infinite Disco
  • Silent tracks or tracks under 1 minute trigger an infinite boss loop in Free Play

The Encore Restoration

Restore your home base to unlock rewards

The Encore is your home base — a music club and bar. You restore it by collecting memorabilia. Full restoration rewards: new Charlie outfits and dance moves, backstory cutscenes, unique weapon skins, and the True Ending path.

Easter Eggs

Hidden surprises await discovery

  • The disco ball in The Encore reflects the current track's album art
  • Typing "CHRLIE1982" in My Music search unlocks developer commentary mode
  • Completing Infinite Disco on hardest difficulty without damage unlocks Ghost Charlie skin
  • Each Idol boss has a fail song — if you die, the music shifts to a mournful version

Achievements & Unlockables

Unlock achievements for exclusive rewards

  • Full Encore — Restore The Encore to maximum level
  • Beat Drop — Land 50 Beat Sync attacks in a single session
  • True Disco — Complete the game with the True Ending
  • Endless Night — Reach rank S+ in Infinite Disco
  • My Music, My Rules — Complete a custom challenge with your own track
