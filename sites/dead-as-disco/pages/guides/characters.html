Characters & Bosses

Meet Charlie Disco and the five Idol betrayers. Each boss has unique attack patterns and a Beat Sync phase.

The Cast

Dead As Disco centers on Charlie Disco and the five former bandmates who betrayed him. Tackle them in any order after the first boss.

Protagonist

Charlie Disco

Drummer of Dead As Disco. Killed ten years ago, resurrected for one night. Fights to uncover the truth and get revenge. Every punch lands on the beat.

Style: Rhythm Combo Difficulty: Balanced
Boss

Arora — The AI Idol

Next-gen AI pop idol built by Harmony Corp. Fires melodic projectiles in rhythm. Boss Phase 2 activates Harmony Trap area denial.

Arena: Digital Stage Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆
Boss

Riff — The Guitarist

Former lead guitarist. Attacks with sound waves and riff combinations. Gets aggressive at 50% HP.

Arena: Concert Hall Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
Boss

Bass — The Bassist

Former bassist. Low-frequency attacks shake the arena. Creates bass drops that knock Charlie back.

Arena: Underground Club Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
Boss

Vox — The Vocalist

Former lead vocalist. Sonic scream attacks and echo traps. Beat Sync phase at 25% HP is extremely dangerous.

Arena: Karaoke Theater Difficulty: ★★★★☆
Boss

Keys — The Keyboardist

Former keyboardist. Creates sonic trap formations and uses synthesized attacks. Controls the battlefield with precision.

Arena: Studio Room Difficulty: ★★★★☆

Boss Strategy Tips

  • Save Beat Sync — Each boss has a Beat Sync phase at 25% HP
  • Watch for pink glow — Indicates a Beat Sync attack is coming
  • Stagger windows — 2-3 seconds after a full combo
  • Grab & throw — Throw bosses into each other to open safe attack windows
