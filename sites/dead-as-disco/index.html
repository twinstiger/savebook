Frequently Asked Questions

Dead As Disco is a rhythm-based Beat 'Em Up where you play as Charlie Disco, the resurrected drummer of legendary band Dead As Disco. Ten years after his murder, his former bandmates reunite for a concert — and Charlie rises from the grave to confront them. It's a nonlinear story of reunion, revenge, and redemption, synced to an original 30+ song soundtrack.
It's a hybrid: a full Beat 'Em Up combat game where punches, kicks, and combos all sync to the music. The better you match the beat, the stronger your combos become. You can also import your own music via the My Music feature, making every song a custom playable level.
Dead As Disco is available on Steam and Epic Games Store. Check the official store pages for system requirements and the latest pricing.
Each boss is a former bandmate who betrayed Charlie: Arora (AI pop idol), plus four other idols with unique attack patterns. Each has a Beat Sync phase in the fight that rewards rhythm timing. Full boss guides are in the Characters section.
The Encore is Charlie's home base — a music club and bar you restore by collecting memorabilia throughout the game. Restoring it fully is tied to unlocking the true story behind Charlie's death and the band's breakup.
